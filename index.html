Two hearts found
each other in the

Both physicians. Both dreamers. What started as meeting on the first day of medical school became a love story neither of us ever expected — and one we never want to end.

The Bride

Shriya Airen, MD

Shriya grew up in Edison, New Jersey and pursued her passion for medicine at the University of Miami. She specializes in ENT and, when she's not in the OR, loves scuba diving and cuddling with her puppy Kal.

The Groom

David Taylor Gonzalez, MD

David grew up in Nicaragua and moved to the US when he was eight years old, settling in Florida. He attended the University of Miami for medical school and now specializes in Ophthalmology.

Chapter One

How We Met

We met on the very first day of medical school at the University of Miami, sorted into the same academic society during orientation. Some things are just meant to be.
