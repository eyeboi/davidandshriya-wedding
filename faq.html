FAQ

You've got questions. We've got answers.

What should I wear?

Each event has its own dress code listed on the Events page. For the ceremony, traditional Indian attire or Western formal wear are both welcome. For Mehendi and Sangeet, colorful and festive outfits are encouraged. Please avoid wearing white or black to the ceremony.

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to venue capacity, we can only accommodate guests listed on the invitation. Please refer to your invitation for details.

Dietary accommodations?

Yes! Our menu includes vegetarian, vegan, and non-vegetarian options. Note any allergies or restrictions when you RSVP.

What is a traditional Indian wedding?

Our ceremony includes the baraat (groom's procession), varmala (garland exchange), pheras (circling the sacred fire), and sindoor & mangalsutra. Conducted in Sanskrit with English explanations so everyone can follow along.

Are kids welcome?

Absolutely. Include children's names when you RSVP so we can plan accordingly.

What is the baraat?

The groom's grand entrance procession with music, dancing, and sometimes a horse. It's one of the most fun parts — everyone is welcome to join.
